introduction

The crime rate in London has always been high, and the variety of crimes has caused London's crime situation to become one of the most concerned things.In this project, I choose the data which counts the number of different crime type in different boroughs of London per month. The research question is to explore the relationship between crime types and crime areas by studying the number of crimes. The data source link is: https://data.london.gov.uk/dataset/recorded_crime_summary
