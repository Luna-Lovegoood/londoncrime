Introduction
  The crime rate in London has always been high, and the variety of crimes has caused London's crime situation to become one of the most concerned things.In this project, I choose the data which counts the number of different crime type in different boroughs of London per month. The research question is to explore the relationship between crime types and crime areas by studying the number of crimes. The data source link is: https://data.london.gov.uk/dataset/recorded_crime_summary
Chart
  The line chart is drawn of crime in different areas of London for users to view it according to their needs. And setting up two filters at the same time to filter by region and crime type.
  The histogram counts the number of crimes in different areas of London according to the type of crime, and you can intuitively see the type of crime with the highest crime rate in London.
Conclusion
  By visualizing the crime data in different areas of London, you can clearly see the crime situation in various areas of London, which is convenient for people to evaluate the security of the area. We can also clearly see which type of crime is most common in the histogram, so people can prevent this in advance. To a certain extent, this has played an effective role in preparing people in advance to prevent crimes from being committed. In addition, there are many areas for improvement in this project. For example, a visual map can be made to make people more clearly see the crime situation in various parts of London.
